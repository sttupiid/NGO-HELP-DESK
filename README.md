# NGO AI Help Desk

An AI-powered help desk system for NGOs that provides instant support to citizens while allowing human agents to take over conversations when needed.

---

## 📋 About the Project

NGO AI Help Desk is a web-based support platform that combines the power of AI with human expertise. Citizens get instant help through an AI chatbot (powered by Google Gemini), and NGO staff can seamlessly take over conversations when complex issues require personal attention.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

NGOs often struggle with:
- **Limited resources** - Can't provide 24/7 support with small teams
- **High volume of inquiries** - Manual handling is time-consuming
- **Response delays** - Citizens wait long for answers
- **No proper tracking** - Difficult to manage complaint history

**Solution:** An AI-powered help desk that handles routine queries instantly while giving human agents tools to manage escalated cases efficiently.

---

## ✨ Features

### For Citizens
-  **Chat with AI** - Get instant help 24/7 using Google Gemini
-  **Talk to Real Person** - Escalate to human agent with one click
-  **Session ID** - Track your conversation
-  **Mobile Friendly** - Works on any device
-  **Multi-language** - Hindi, English, Hinglish support

### For NGO Staff
-  **Agent Dashboard** - View all incoming escalated chats
-  **Real-time Chat** - Reply to citizens instantly
-  **Status Management** - Track conversation progress
-  **Session Search** - Find conversations easily
-  **Complaint Logging** - Register formal complaints
